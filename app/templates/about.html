About

I'm Gustav Louv. I build AI systems that automate how companies sell.

Six months ago, I founded Agent360—the Nordic AI sales infrastructure. We're building voice agents that understand context, navigate complexity, and close deals. Not chatbots. Not scripts. Systems that think.

Early Days: Sales Floors

Before 25, I closed 8,000+ electricity contracts. Door-to-door, cold calls, objection handling—the full grind. I learned what actually moves people to buy: context, timing, and trust.

Sales isn't about talking faster. It's about listening better and responding with precision. That insight became the foundation for everything I'd build later.

Tenerife: First Ventures

I moved to Tenerife and started experimenting with business models—small ventures, rapid iteration, learning what worked and what didn't. The goal wasn't scale yet. It was understanding how to build systems that generated value without constant manual input.

The EcommerceBot Saga

Then came EcommerceBot. Built it with a co-founder. Market validation looked good. Then the hostage situation hit—my co-founder held the codebase and infrastructure hostage. Everything locked down overnight.

I moved to San Francisco to rebuild. Found a new co-founder. Pivoted the product toward Shopify. Learned hard lessons about partnerships, control, and resilience. The rebuild worked, but the experience taught me more about what not to do than what to do.

The AI Shift

During the rebuild, I saw where the world was heading. AI wasn't a feature—it was the infrastructure. Sales teams weren't going to keep scaling linearly with headcount. The winners would be the ones who built AI-native sales systems.

I started focusing full-time on AI. Not the hype. Not the demos. The actual engineering required to make agents that could sell at scale. Context-aware. Tool-equipped. Adaptive.

Agent360: Now

Six months ago, I founded Agent360. We're building the top AI-powered sales voice solution in the Nordics. Our agents understand local nuance, regulatory requirements, and buyer behavior.

The mission: automate how the Nordics sell. Not replace salespeople—make exceptional performance repeatable. Turn individual mastery into scalable infrastructure.

We're early, but the trajectory is clear. The next decade of sales belongs to the operators who build the best AI systems. That's what we're doing.
